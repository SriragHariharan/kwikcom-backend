# Microservices Overview - Plan

## Express.js — Commerce Core (Cart Service)
- Cart management
- Order creation & lifecycle
- Apply coupons & discounts
- Checkout orchestration

## FastAPI — Catalog & Inventory (Inventory Service)
- Product CRUD
- Categories & brands
- Search & filtering
- Stock check
- Low-stock alerts

## Spring Boot — Identity & Admin (Auth Service)
- User registration & login (JWT)
- Role & permission management (USER / ADMIN)
- Approve / block users
- Enable / disable products
- Coupon creation, update & deletion (admin only)
